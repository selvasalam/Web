KMCT INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY & MANAGEMENT

KMCT Group of Institutions is one of the largest groups of educational institutions in Kerala, with campuses spanning across Calicut and Malappuram districts. The campuses, set against the idyllic landscapes of Manassery and Kuttippuram, offers students a safe haven to pursue their education. The KMCT Medical College, Kozhikode is a top university located in the Keralan city of Kozhikode. The institute provides full-time PG, Post-Doctorate, and UG courses in the form of degree programs. The most sought-after programs offered by KMCT Medical College, Kozhikode are DNB, MBBS, MD, and MS. These NMC-approved programs are available for students to select from in order to further their academic careers. The institute provides courses in the health sciences and medicine programs. The institute can accommodate 185.
